# Privacy Policy for UkulelePro

**Last Updated:** February 18, 2026

## 1. Introduction
UkulelePro ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Data Collection
We do **not** collect, store, or share any personal data, usage data, or location data.

## 3. Microphone Usage
Our app requires access to your device's microphone solely for the purpose of:
*   Real-time pitch detection (Tuner)
*   Chord recognition (Chord Detector)

**All audio processing is performed locally on your device.** No audio data is ever recorded, saved, or transmitted to any external server.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that collect user data.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
